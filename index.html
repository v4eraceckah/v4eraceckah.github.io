About the app

Vitae is a privacy-focused, decentralized, open source crypto currency run by a global community run by creators, innovators, and technology enthusiasts.
Features-
* Fast Transactions- With 45 seconds block time, the transactions are almost instant.
* Light Weight- Doesn't download full block chain on your device.
* Standalone - Doesn't require dedicated centralized server to work.
* Inbuilt Exchange- Vitae can be directly exchanged to BTC.
* Additional security with PIN.
* QR based transactions.
* Mnemonics for back up of your wallet.
* User friendly.
